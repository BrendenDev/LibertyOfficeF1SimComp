Drivers' Championship

  • Drivers compete individually for the fastest lap time.
  • Lap times remain hidden during active races to ensure competitive fairness.
  • When the race window closes, all times are revealed and points are awarded to the top 10 drivers.
  • Season ties are resolved by the highest number of 1st-place race wins.
🔧

Constructors' Championship

  • Drivers concurrently score points for their 3-person constructor team.
  • Only the top 2 lap times per team contribute to constructor points. The slowest time is discarded from the scoring pool.
  • Points are awarded to the remaining eligible drivers based on the standard Points System.
  • Season ties are resolved by the team's total number of race wins.

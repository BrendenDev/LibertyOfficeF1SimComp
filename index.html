Lap Time Submission

  • Each driver may submit one lap time per race — no resubmissions or overwrites.
  • Times must be entered in Minutes : Seconds . Milliseconds format.
  • Submissions are accepted only during the active race window (between the race start and end date).
  • Make sure to select the correct race before submitting — mistakes cannot be corrected without admin intervention.

Drivers' Championship

  • All drivers compete individually across all races.
  • Points accumulate from every race using the standard points system above.
  • In the event of a points tie, the driver with more wins ranks higher.
  • Lap times are hidden on the race board until the race window closes — results reveal only after the end date.
🔧

Constructors' Championship

  • Teams consist of 3 drivers each.
  • All 3 drivers may submit times and compete in the Drivers' Championship.
  • However, only the top 2 drivers per team (by lap time) count toward the Constructors' Championship per race.
  • Constructor points are re-ranked from the filtered field — not inherited from the full driver race. The fastest eligible driver across all teams gets P1 constructor points, and so on.
  • In the event of a points tie, the team with more race wins (constructor wins) ranks higher.
📅

Race Schedule

  • Each race has a start date and an end date.
  • Upcoming — race window has not opened yet. Submissions are not accepted.
  • Active — race is currently open. Submit your lap time now.
  • Completed — race window has closed. Times, gaps, and points are now revealed.
